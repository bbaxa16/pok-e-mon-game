Welcome to Pokémon: Baxa Edition

    Instructions
  • Press start from the home screen
  • Press ready to battle button and your random pokémon battle buddy will appear!
  • Press battle and your opponent will appear, and it's time to battle!
  • To attack your opponent press your attack button in your pokémon specs in the bottom left corner
  • watch for blinking red borders: If the message console is blinking red, click it!
  • The goal is to outlast your opponent in each battle
  • To win the game you must win 3 battles
  • If you want to restart the game, double click on the buttons
